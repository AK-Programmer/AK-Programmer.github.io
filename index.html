About Me

I'm a 16 year old full-stack developer and AI enthusiast who loves building things. I've worked on several machine learning projects, including a generative (LSTM) chatbot, an image generation model, and a relational reasoning model. I've also worked on several full-stack projects, including a simple note-taking app.

I've spoken at world-class conferences like Elevate, and have also worked on other projects, including consulting challenges for companies like Walmart, Wealthsimple, and Fasken LLP, as well as a SaaS product for a FinTech company. I'm an Activator at The Knowledge Society, a human accelerator for ambitious teens that focuses on developing people who go on to impact billions.

My focus right now is on developing my technical skills (full-stack, machine learning) so that I can build interesting projects. In my free time, I like to code, read about just about anything, and explore the world around me. Click here to see my full portfolio.
