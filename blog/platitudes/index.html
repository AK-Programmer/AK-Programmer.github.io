Useful Platitudes

October 9, 2020

Platitudes get a bad rap, often seen as being of little substance. But I think some of these ostensibly trite statements contain time-tested knowledge that is currently under-appreciated in our world, judging by how many people actually seem to live by them. Here are some of my favourite.

  • Two wrongs don’t make a right.
  • Treat others the way you want to be treated.
  • What goes around comes around.
  • The road to hell is paved with good intentions.
  • Rome wasn’t built in a day.
  • Things could be worse.
  • You’ll get over it.
  • You still have your health (unless you don’t).

Of course, none of these platitudes are always strictly true, but it’s probably still a good idea to live by them in most aspects of your life.
